What is the Coffee Challenge?

The Coffee Challenge is a simple way for students and supporters to make a real impact. By skipping one cafe drink each week and donating that amount, you help fund clean water projects for families in need. Invite friends to join and multiply your impact!

  • Skip one coffee or cafe drink per week
  • Donate the amount you would have spent
  • Challenge three friends to join you
  • Track your impact and share your story
